Bún Thịt Nướng

A warm salad of grilled pork served on cold, cooked noodles. Layers of lettuce and cold rice vermicelli are covered with grilled meat, cucumber, fresh herbs and chopped peanuts, then topped with nuoc cham (dipping sauce).

Ingredients

  • Fish Sauce
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sugar
  • Pork
  • Rice Vermicelli Noodles
  • Cucumbers and Pickled Carrots
  • Letuce and other herbs.
  • Bean Sprouts
  • Peanuts

Steps

  1. Marinate the pork.
  2. Cook the pork and noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Serve the pork on top of the noodles, sliced cucumbers, and pickled carrots. Garnish with crushed peanuts and green onions if desired.
  5. Enjoy!
